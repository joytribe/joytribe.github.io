Privacy Policy for Joye Pad

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Joye Pad is a virtual music pad app designed to help users play notes, chords, scales, and guided songs on a mobile device. This Privacy Policy explains how Joye Pad handles information when you use the app.

1. Information Joye Pad Handles

Joye Pad does not require you to create an account and does not operate its own cloud server for storing your performance activity.

The app may handle the following information on your device:

  • App settings such as selected pad layout, octave setting, master volume, black key display, and guide tempo.
  • Song guide settings such as whether guide mode is enabled, the selected built-in song, and the current guide position.
  • Touch input used to play notes, chords, sustain, octave controls, and guide steps.
  • Basic device and app information used by third-party advertising services, as described below.

2. Music Playback and Song Guide Data

Joye Pad generates musical notes and chords on your device for real-time playback. The app uses your touch input to play sounds, highlight notes, advance the song guide, and control features such as sustain, octave changes, and automatic guided playback.

Built-in song guide content and practice layouts are included in the app. Your selected song, guide tempo, layout choice, volume setting, black key preference, and related preferences are stored locally on your device. Joye Pad does not upload your played notes, guide progress, or practice activity to a server controlled by us.

3. Audio

Joye Pad outputs generated audio through your device speaker, headphones, or other audio output selected by your system. The app does not request microphone permission and does not record audio from your device.

4. Advertising

Joye Pad uses Google AdMob to show ads, including app open ads. Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and share information such as device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, app interactions, diagnostics, and other data needed for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service improvement.

Google may use this information in accordance with its own policies. You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies
  • Google AdMob Privacy and Messaging

You can manage or reset your advertising ID and ad personalization choices in your Android device settings where available.

5. Permissions

Joye Pad may use the following Android permissions:

  • Internet access: used for advertising services and related network functions.
  • Network state access: used to determine whether network access is available.

Joye Pad does not request microphone, camera, contacts, location, or file storage permissions.

6. Data Sharing

Joye Pad does not sell your personal information. Joye Pad does not share your local settings, played notes, song guide progress, or practice activity with us.

Data may be shared with third-party services only as needed for features you use, such as Google AdMob advertising.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

App settings, selected song guide options, and related preferences are stored locally on your device for as long as the app remains installed or until you remove the data.

You can delete locally stored app data by clearing Joye Pad's app data in Android settings or by uninstalling the app. Data handled by third-party services is subject to those services' own retention and deletion practices.

8. Children

Joye Pad is not directed to children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through third-party services used by the app, please contact us and the relevant third-party service provider.

9. International Data Transfers

Joye Pad stores app settings and practice preferences locally on your device. Third-party services such as Google AdMob may process information on servers located in different countries or regions according to their own policies.

10. Security

Joye Pad is designed to keep your music practice settings and guide progress on your device. However, no app or method of electronic storage is completely secure. You are responsible for managing access to your device.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Joye Pad after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Joye Pad's privacy practices, contact us at: joyetribe@163.com
