Privacy Policy for JoyeGo

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Last updated: July 21, 2026

JoyeGo is a Go learning and training app that provides interactive lessons and on-device games against a bundled Go engine. Its teaching, gameplay, scoring, and Go-engine features operate locally. Internet access is used only to request and display Google AdMob app open ads. This Privacy Policy explains how JoyeGo handles information when you use the app.

1. Information JoyeGo Handles

JoyeGo does not require an account and does not operate its own cloud server for collecting or storing user information or gameplay. The app does not ask you to provide a name, email address, phone number, photo, audio recording, contacts, precise location, payment information, or other personal information.

The app handles only the following limited information:

  • Unfinished-game data, including board size, selected difficulty, stone positions, move number, current player, captures, passes, the last move, and position history.
  • Temporary lesson, screen, and gameplay state needed to provide the app's teaching and training features.
  • A local preference recording whether sound effects are enabled.
  • A local timestamp recording when an app open ad was last shown, used only to control ad frequency.
  • Bundled course content, language resources, sound files, and Go-engine files required to run the app.
  • Information processed by Google AdMob when an ad is requested, as described below.

2. Offline Gameplay and Go-Engine Processing

JoyeGo processes lessons, board positions, scoring, move legality, computer moves, animations, and sound effects locally on your device. Board positions used by the bundled KataGo-based engine are processed on the device and are not uploaded to us, Google AdMob, or an online Go service.

The teaching and training features remain available without a network connection. If an ad cannot be loaded, JoyeGo continues to the main screen without requiring the user to connect. JoyeGo does not provide account, social-network, cloud-sync, or online-match services and does not make in-app purchases available.

3. Local App Data and Android Backup

JoyeGo automatically stores an unfinished training game in the app's private storage so that you can continue it later. Completed games are not retained as unfinished games. A newer game may replace an earlier saved game. The sound-effects setting is stored locally so that the app can remember your preference. The app also stores the time when an app open ad was last shown to avoid displaying ads too frequently. This timestamp does not directly identify the user and is not uploaded to a server controlled by us.

The bundled Go model may be copied from the installed app package into private app storage so that the offline engine can run. This model is application content and does not contain user information.

Depending on your Android device, account, backup provider, and backup settings, Android may back up and restore some private app data. Any system-level backup is controlled by your device and backup provider and is governed by their settings and privacy policies. JoyeGo itself does not transmit saved games, preferences, or Go-engine data to a server operated by us.

4. Advertising

JoyeGo uses Google AdMob to show app open ads. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share information such as IP address, general location derived from IP address, app interactions, diagnostic information, and device, app, or account-related identifiers for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service operation.

JoyeGo configures every ad request for child age treatment, requests non-personalized ads, and limits ad content to Google's G rating. JoyeGo also removes the Android advertising ID and Privacy Sandbox advertising permissions from the app. Google states that personalized advertising and remarketing are disabled and the Android Advertising ID is not transmitted for ad requests marked for child age treatment. These settings limit advertising data use but do not prevent Google from processing all network, interaction, diagnostic, or fraud-prevention information.

Google processes information under its own policies. You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK Data Disclosure
  • Google AdMob Age-Restricted Treatment

5. Permissions

JoyeGo may use the following Android permissions:

  • Internet access: used to request and display Google AdMob ads.
  • Network state access: used by advertising services to determine whether network access is available.
  • Wake lock: declared by the Google Mobile Ads SDK to support ad loading and display.
  • Foreground service: declared by the Google Mobile Ads SDK for advertising service operation. JoyeGo does not use it to run the Go engine or collect gameplay.

JoyeGo does not request camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, phone, SMS, calendar, nearby-device, advertising-ID, or broad photo, media, and file-access permissions. Playing bundled sound effects does not require microphone access.

6. Third-Party Components

JoyeGo includes Google Mobile Ads SDK for advertising and open-source software components such as KataGo and Eigen for offline Go move generation. KataGo and Eigen run as part of the app on your device and are not integrated as online services or data-collection SDKs.

The app store, Android operating system, device manufacturer, or backup provider may process installation, update, diagnostic, security, or backup information independently under their own terms and privacy policies. We do not control information handled independently by those services.

7. Data Sharing and Sale

JoyeGo does not sell personal information. We do not operate a server that receives names, contact details, board positions, lesson activity, saved games, photos, audio, or precise location, and the app does not intentionally provide those items to Google AdMob.

Information may be collected and shared by Google AdMob as necessary to request, deliver, measure, secure, and improve advertising services, subject to the child-directed configuration described above and Google's own policies.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

An unfinished game remains in private app storage until it is completed, replaced, cleared with the app's data, or removed when the app is uninstalled. The sound-effects preference and local ad-display timestamp remain until they are changed or removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. Data restored by an Android backup is controlled through the relevant device and backup provider settings.

Information processed by Google AdMob is subject to Google's retention and deletion practices. Requests concerning information handled by Google should be directed to Google using the privacy controls and contact methods described in Google's policies.

9. Children's Privacy

JoyeGo is a general-audience learning app that may also be used by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. The app does not include account registration, public profiles, chat, social features, user-content uploads, or in-app purchases.

Advertising requests are marked for child age treatment, are non-personalized, and are limited to G-rated ad content. If you are a parent or guardian and believe personal information from a child has been collected through JoyeGo, please contact us. If the information was handled by Google AdMob, you may also need to contact Google directly.

10. International Data Transfers

JoyeGo keeps its locally stored gameplay data and preferences on the device and does not transfer them to a server controlled by us. Google AdMob may process information on servers located in different countries or regions in accordance with Google's policies and applicable law.

11. Security

JoyeGo minimizes data handling by processing teaching and gameplay locally, avoiding accounts and sensitive permissions, and storing unfinished-game data and preferences in Android's private app storage. No method of electronic storage or network transmission is completely secure, and third-party services apply their own security practices.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when JoyeGo's features, data practices, third-party services, distribution methods, or legal requirements change. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, JoyeGo's privacy practices, or a child's information, contact us at: joyetribe@163.com
