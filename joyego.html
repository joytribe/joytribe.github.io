Privacy Policy for JoyeGo

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Last updated: July 19, 2026

JoyeGo is an offline Go learning and training app that provides interactive lessons and on-device games against a bundled Go engine. This Privacy Policy explains how JoyeGo handles information when you use the app.

1. Information JoyeGo Handles

JoyeGo does not require an account and does not operate a cloud server for collecting or storing user information. The app does not ask you to provide a name, email address, phone number, photo, audio recording, contacts, precise location, payment information, or other personal information.

The app handles only the following limited information on your device:

  • Unfinished-game data, including board size, selected difficulty, stone positions, move number, current player, captures, passes, the last move, and position history.
  • Temporary lesson, screen, and gameplay state needed to provide the app's teaching and training features.
  • Bundled course content, language resources, and Go-engine files required to run the app.

2. Offline Gameplay and Go-Engine Processing

JoyeGo processes lessons, board positions, scoring, move legality, and computer moves locally on your device. Board positions used by the bundled KataGo-based engine are processed on the device and are not uploaded to us or sent to an online Go service.

The app does not declare the Android internet permission and does not include advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, account, social-network, or cloud-sync services. JoyeGo does not display ads and does not make in-app purchases available.

3. Local App Data and Android Backup

JoyeGo automatically stores an unfinished training game in the app's private storage so that you can continue it later. This information is used only to restore the game on your device. Completed games are not retained as unfinished games. A newer game may replace an earlier saved game.

The bundled Go model may be copied from the installed app package into private app storage so that the offline engine can run. This model is application content and does not contain user information.

Depending on your Android device, account, backup provider, and backup settings, Android may back up and restore some private app data, such as the saved unfinished game. Any system-level backup is controlled by your device and backup provider and is governed by their settings and privacy policies. JoyeGo itself does not transmit this data to a server operated by us.

4. Permissions

JoyeGo does not request internet, camera, microphone, contacts, location, phone, SMS, calendar, nearby-device, advertising-ID, or broad photo, media, and file-access permissions.

5. Third-Party Components

JoyeGo includes open-source software components such as KataGo and Eigen to provide offline Go move generation. These components run as part of the app on your device and are not integrated as online services or data-collection SDKs.

The app store, Android operating system, device manufacturer, or backup provider may process installation, update, diagnostic, security, or backup information independently under their own terms and privacy policies. We do not control information handled independently by those services.

6. Data Sharing and Sale

JoyeGo does not sell, rent, share, or transmit personal information or gameplay data to us, advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or other third parties. We do not operate a server that receives your board positions, lesson activity, saved games, device identifiers, or contact details.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

An unfinished game remains in private app storage until it is completed, replaced, cleared with the app's data, or removed when the app is uninstalled. You can delete locally stored JoyeGo data at any time by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app. Data restored by an Android backup is controlled through the relevant device and backup provider settings.

8. Children's Privacy

JoyeGo is a general-audience learning app that may also be used by children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. It does not provide account registration, public profiles, chat, social features, user-content uploads, advertising, or in-app purchases.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that personal information has been collected through JoyeGo, please contact us using the address below.

9. International Data Transfers

JoyeGo does not transfer app or gameplay data to a server controlled by us in any country or region. Android system services, app stores, device manufacturers, and backup providers may process information in different countries or regions according to their own policies and your settings.

10. Security

JoyeGo minimizes privacy risk by working offline, avoiding accounts and sensitive permissions, and storing unfinished-game data in Android's private app storage. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, and the security of device-level storage and backups also depends on your device, operating system, account, and backup-provider settings.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when JoyeGo's features, data practices, third-party components, distribution methods, or legal requirements change. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or JoyeGo's privacy practices, contact us at: joyetribe@163.com
