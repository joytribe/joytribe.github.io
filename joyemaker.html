Privacy Policy for Joye Maker

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Joye Maker is a child-directed creativity and learning app that provides step-by-step drawing, craft, recycling, and safe science activities for children and families. This Privacy Policy explains how Joye Maker handles information when you use the app.

1. Information Joye Maker Handles

Joye Maker does not require an account and does not operate its own cloud server for storing children's information, activity, or creations. The app does not ask users to provide a name, email address, phone number, photo, audio recording, contacts, or precise location.

The app handles only the following limited information:

  • Bundled project instructions, illustrations, and language content needed to use the app.
  • A local timestamp recording when an app open ad was last shown, used only to control ad frequency.
  • Information processed by Google AdMob when an ad is requested, as described below.

2. Creative Projects and App Content

Project instructions and illustrations are included with the app and can be viewed without creating an account or uploading information. Joye Maker does not provide a project gallery, social feed, chat feature, or other user-generated-content service. The app does not capture, save, or upload children's drawings, craft photos, experiment records, or other creations.

3. Local App Data

Joye Maker stores the time when an app open ad was last displayed in private app storage. This value is used only to avoid showing app open ads too frequently. It does not identify the user and is not uploaded to a server controlled by us.

Depending on your Android device and backup settings, Android may back up and restore limited app data. Android system backup services are governed by the device or service provider's own privacy policy and settings.

4. Advertising

Joye Maker uses Google AdMob to show app open ads. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share information such as IP address, general location derived from IP address, app interactions, diagnostic information, and device, app, or account-related identifiers for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service operation.

Joye Maker configures every ad request for child age treatment, requests non-personalized ads, and limits ad content to Google's G rating. Joye Maker also removes the Android advertising ID permission from the app. Google states that personalized advertising and remarketing are disabled and the Android Advertising ID is not transmitted for ad requests marked for child age treatment. These settings limit advertising data use but do not prevent Google from processing all network, interaction, diagnostic, or fraud-prevention information.

Google processes information under its own policies. You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK Data Disclosure
  • Google AdMob Age-Restricted Treatment

5. Permissions

Joye Maker may use the following Android permissions:

  • Internet access: used to request and display Google AdMob ads.
  • Network state access: used by advertising services to determine whether network access is available.

Joye Maker does not request camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, or broad photo and file access permissions.

6. Data Sharing

Joye Maker does not sell personal information. We do not operate a server that receives children's names, contact details, creations, photos, audio, precise location, or project completion records, and the app does not intentionally provide those items to Google AdMob.

Information may be collected and shared by Google AdMob as necessary to request, deliver, measure, secure, and improve advertising services, subject to the child-directed configuration described above and Google's own policies.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

The local ad-display timestamp remains on the device until app data is cleared, the app is uninstalled, or Android backup settings cause it to be restored. You can delete locally stored Joye Maker data by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

Information processed by Google AdMob is subject to Google's retention and deletion practices. Requests concerning information handled by Google should be directed to Google using the privacy controls and contact methods described in Google's policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Joye Maker is designed for children and families. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. The app does not include account registration, social features, direct messaging, public profiles, user content uploads, or in-app purchases.

Advertising requests are marked for child age treatment, are non-personalized, and are limited to G-rated ad content. If you are a parent or guardian and believe personal information from a child has been collected through Joye Maker, please contact us. If the information was handled by Google AdMob, you may also need to contact Google directly.

9. International Data Transfers

Joye Maker keeps its limited app data on the device and does not transfer it to a server controlled by us. Google AdMob may process information on servers located in different countries or regions in accordance with Google's policies and applicable law.

10. Security

Joye Maker minimizes data handling by providing its creative content locally and by avoiding accounts, uploads, and operator-controlled cloud storage. No method of electronic storage or network transmission is completely secure, and third-party services apply their own security practices.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app's features, data practices, third-party services, or legal requirements change. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, Joye Maker's privacy practices, or a child's information, contact us at: joyetribe@163.com
