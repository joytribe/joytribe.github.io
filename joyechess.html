Privacy Policy for Joye Chess

Effective date: July 21, 2026

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Joye Chess is a chess learning and training app that provides interactive lessons, tactical puzzles, and on-device games against a built-in chess engine. Its teaching, chess-rules, gameplay, and computer-opponent features operate locally. Internet access is used only to request and display Google AdMob app open ads. This Privacy Policy explains how Joye Chess handles information when you use the app.

1. Information Joye Chess Handles

Joye Chess does not require an account and does not operate its own cloud server for collecting or storing user information, lesson activity, puzzles, or games. The app does not ask you to provide a name, email address, phone number, photo, audio recording, contacts, precise location, payment information, or other personal information.

The app handles only the following limited information:

  • Unfinished-game data, including the chess position, side to move, castling and en-passant state, move counters, selected difficulty, and the side controlled by the user.
  • Local progress identifiers for completed interactive lessons and solved tactical puzzles.
  • Temporary lesson, puzzle, screen, animation, and gameplay state needed to provide the app's learning and playing features.
  • Local preferences recording whether sound effects are enabled and whether the app's default language-following setup has been applied.
  • A local timestamp recording when an app open ad was last shown, used only to control ad frequency.
  • Bundled lesson and puzzle content, language resources, chess-piece graphics, and sound files required to run the app.
  • Information processed by Google AdMob when an ad is requested, as described below.

2. Offline Learning, Puzzles, and Chess Processing

Joye Chess processes lessons, tactical positions, move legality, computer moves, game status, animations, and sound effects locally on your device. Chess positions are processed by the built-in chess engine on the device and are not uploaded to us, Google AdMob, or an online chess service.

The teaching, tactical-training, and computer-game features remain available without a network connection. If an ad cannot be loaded, Joye Chess continues to the main screen without requiring the user to connect. Joye Chess does not provide account, public-profile, chat, social-network, cloud-sync, online-match, or in-app-purchase services.

3. Local App Data and Android Backup

Joye Chess automatically stores an unfinished chess game in the app's private storage so that you can continue it later. Completed games are removed from the unfinished-game slot, and a newer game may replace an earlier saved game. The app stores completed lesson identifiers, solved puzzle identifiers, the sound-effects setting, and a language-setup flag so that it can remember progress and preferences. It also stores the time when an app open ad was last shown to avoid displaying ads too frequently. These local values do not directly identify the user and are not uploaded to a server controlled by us.

Depending on your Android device, account, backup provider, and backup settings, Android may back up and restore some private app data. Any system-level backup is controlled by your device and backup provider and is governed by their settings and privacy policies. Joye Chess itself does not transmit saved games, learning progress, puzzle progress, or preferences to a server operated by us.

4. Advertising

Joye Chess uses Google AdMob to show app open ads. According to Google's Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure, the SDK may automatically collect and share information such as IP address, general location estimated from IP address, user interactions with the app or ads, diagnostic information, and device, app, or account-related identifiers for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service operation. Google states that this data is encrypted in transit.

Joye Chess configures every ad request for child age treatment, requests non-personalized ads, and limits ad content to Google's G rating. Joye Chess also removes the Android advertising ID and Privacy Sandbox advertising permissions from the app. Google states that child age treatment disables personalized advertising and remarketing, disables requests to third-party ad vendors, applies child ad-serving protections, and prevents the Android Advertising Identifier from being transmitted. These settings limit advertising data use but do not prevent Google from processing all network, interaction, diagnostic, security, or fraud-prevention information.

Google processes information under its own policies. You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK Data Disclosure
  • Google AdMob Age-Restricted Treatment

5. Permissions

Joye Chess may use the following Android permissions:

  • Internet access: used to request and display Google AdMob ads.
  • Network state access: used by advertising services to determine whether network access is available.
  • Wake lock: declared by the Google Mobile Ads SDK to support ad loading and display.
  • Foreground service: declared by the Google Mobile Ads SDK for advertising service operation. Joye Chess does not use it to run the chess engine or collect gameplay.

Joye Chess does not request camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, phone, SMS, calendar, nearby-device, advertising-ID, or broad photo, media, and file-access permissions. Playing bundled sound effects does not require microphone access.

6. Third-Party Components

Joye Chess includes Google Mobile Ads SDK for advertising and open-source software components such as AndroidX, Jetpack Compose, Kotlin, and Kotlin Coroutines for application functionality. The chess rules, chess engine, lessons, and tactical puzzles run as part of the app on your device and are not integrated as online data-collection services.

The app store, Android operating system, device manufacturer, or backup provider may process installation, update, diagnostic, security, or backup information independently under their own terms and privacy policies. We do not control information handled independently by those services.

7. Data Sharing and Sale

Joye Chess does not sell personal information. We do not operate a server that receives names, contact details, chess positions, lesson activity, puzzle progress, saved games, photos, audio, or precise location, and the app does not intentionally provide those items to Google AdMob.

Information may be collected and shared by Google AdMob as necessary to request, deliver, measure, secure, and improve advertising services, subject to the child age treatment and non-personalized configuration described above and Google's own policies.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

An unfinished game remains in private app storage until it is completed, replaced, cleared with the app's data, or removed when the app is uninstalled. Lesson and puzzle completion identifiers, the sound-effects preference, the language-setup flag, and the local ad-display timestamp remain until they are changed or removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. Data restored by an Android backup is controlled through the relevant device and backup provider settings.

Information processed by Google AdMob is subject to Google's retention and deletion practices. Requests concerning information handled by Google should be directed to Google using the privacy controls and contact methods described in Google's policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Joye Chess is a general-audience learning app that may also be used by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. The app does not include account registration, public profiles, chat, social features, user-content uploads, or in-app purchases.

Advertising requests are marked for child age treatment, are non-personalized, and are limited to G-rated ad content. If you are a parent or guardian and believe personal information from a child has been collected through Joye Chess, please contact us. If the information was handled by Google AdMob, you may also need to contact Google directly.

10. International Data Transfers

Joye Chess keeps its locally stored game data, learning progress, puzzle progress, and preferences on the device and does not transfer them to a server controlled by us. Google AdMob may process information on servers located in different countries or regions in accordance with Google's policies and applicable law.

11. Security

Joye Chess minimizes data handling by processing teaching, puzzles, and gameplay locally, avoiding accounts and sensitive permissions, and storing unfinished-game data, progress, and preferences in Android's private app storage. No method of electronic storage or network transmission is completely secure, and third-party services apply their own security practices.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when Joye Chess's features, data practices, third-party services, distribution methods, or legal requirements change. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.

13. Contact Us
