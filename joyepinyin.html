Privacy Policy for JoyePinyin

Effective date: June 30, 2026

Last updated: June 30, 2026

JoyePinyin is an EPUB reading app designed to help users read Chinese books with pinyin assistance, reading progress, bookshelf management, and translation-related features. This Privacy Policy explains how JoyePinyin handles information when you use the app.

1. Information JoyePinyin Handles

JoyePinyin does not require you to create an account and does not operate its own cloud server for storing your books or reading activity.

The app may handle the following information on your device:

  • EPUB files and folders that you choose to open or scan.
  • Book metadata such as title, cover image, chapter count, file name, and file location.
  • Bookshelf records, reading progress, last opened chapter, and reading display preferences.
  • Chinese text selected by you for pinyin lookup or translation.
  • Basic device and app information used by third-party advertising services, as described below.

2. EPUB Files and Reading Data

When you open an EPUB file or scan a folder for EPUB files, JoyePinyin reads the files that you explicitly select or authorize through the Android file picker. The app uses this access to display books, extract cover images, build your bookshelf, and restore your reading progress.

Your EPUB content, bookshelf records, covers, reading progress, and reading settings are stored locally on your device. JoyePinyin does not upload your EPUB files or reading history to a server controlled by us.

3. Pinyin Dictionary

JoyePinyin includes local pinyin conversion to display pinyin above Chinese characters. Pinyin lookup is performed locally on your device.

4. Translation Features

JoyePinyin may offer translation-related features when you select Chinese text. Depending on your device and your actions, translation may be handled by the Android system translation service or by an external translation app installed on your device.

If you choose to translate selected text, that text may be sent to the system translation service or the external translation app you select. Those services are provided by third parties or your device system, and their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies and settings.

5. Advertising

JoyePinyin uses Google AdMob to show ads, including app open ads. Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and share information such as device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, app interactions, diagnostics, and other data needed for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service improvement.

Google may use this information in accordance with its own policies. You can learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies
  • Google AdMob Privacy and Messaging

You can manage or reset your advertising ID and ad personalization choices in your Android device settings where available.

6. Permissions

JoyePinyin may use the following Android permissions:

  • Internet access: used for advertising services and related network functions.
  • Network state access: used to determine whether network access is available.
  • File or folder access selected by you: used to open and scan EPUB files through Android system file access.

7. Data Sharing

JoyePinyin does not sell your personal information. JoyePinyin does not share your EPUB files or local reading history with us.

Data may be shared with third-party services only as needed for features you use, such as Google AdMob advertising, Android system translation, or an external translation app selected by you.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Bookshelf data, reading progress, cached book information, and reading preferences are stored locally on your device for as long as the app remains installed or until you remove the data.

You can remove books from the bookshelf inside the app where available. You can also delete locally stored app data by clearing JoyePinyin's app data in Android settings or by uninstalling the app. Data handled by third-party services is subject to those services' own retention and deletion practices.

9. Children

JoyePinyin is not directed to children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through third-party services used by the app, please contact us and the relevant third-party service provider.

10. International Data Transfers

JoyePinyin stores app reading data locally on your device. Third-party services such as Google AdMob or translation services may process information on servers located in different countries or regions according to their own policies.

11. Security

JoyePinyin is designed to keep your books and reading progress on your device. However, no app or method of electronic storage is completely secure. You are responsible for managing access to your device and files.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of JoyePinyin after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or JoyePinyin's privacy practices, contact us at: joyetribe@163.com
